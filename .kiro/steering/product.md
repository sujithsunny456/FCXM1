# Product: Focal CXM Job Seeker App

## Overview
A job seeker application built under the **Focal CXM** brand ("Remarkable" tagline). The app helps job seekers discover opportunities, manage applications, and connect with employers — all within a polished, on-brand experience.

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name:** Focal CXM
- **Tagline:** Remarkable
- **Logo:** Use the official Focal CXM logo (Midnight Blue wordmark with "REMARKABLE" subtitle). Never distort, recolor, or modify the logo. Always maintain clear space around it.

## Core User Goals
- Browse and search job listings
- Create and manage a job seeker profile
- Apply to jobs and track application status
- Receive relevant job recommendations
